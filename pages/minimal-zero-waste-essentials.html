This is a revised version of a blog post for ZeroWaste.Org

what is the minimal set of items you need to have a successful zero waste day?

{
  • container for wet things (thermos/mug can handle any temperature)
  • fabric bag for dry things (doubles as a napkin)
  • a versatile utensil (perhaps a spork?)
  • tote bag to hold your essentials (and other stuff too)
}

And I think you can do pretty well with that (as long as you're willing to occasionally get a little messy).
